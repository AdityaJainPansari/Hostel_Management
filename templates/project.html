About IIIT-H hostels

Posted on April 7, 2018 by forumiiith

 

IIIT-H Hostel portal has been setup and maintained by the student body at IIIT-Hyderabad to ease the managing of the hostels by the Hostel Com.2018, as well as increase the healthy interaction among students for issues related to their Wings , Floors , Hostels ...

This is a forum by the students, for the students.

We try our best to give unbiased and impartial opportunity to everyone's queries.

On this portal, posts by Portal administrators & CoW may be considered authentic and trust-worthy. However, there may be other users who will try and solve a lot of your issues.

We will not entertain any spam posts, posts with abusive language and irrelevant posts.

Hope this portal helps you.
